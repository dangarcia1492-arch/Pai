Dashboard variants — General · EU · US

Three variants of the member dashboard, tuned by jurisdiction scope. Scope-default at sign-in (no per-user prefs yet). Same skeleton, scope-specific hero, modules and sidebar.

Variant 1 · default

General dashboard

Customers without a single-country focus. Global feed, region jumper, tracker entry-points by scope.

Hero · "What's new across your watchlist" Modules · activity feed · 90d deadlines Sidebar · 6 tracker entry-points
Variant 2

EU member-state

Watchlist on EU 27 + EU institutions. AI Act / NIS2 / GDPR aggregate progress, deadline urgency, EDPB activity.

Hero · "EU regulatory pulse" + urgency band Modules · EU 27 implementation strip · EDPB feed Sidebar · AI Act countdown · NIS2 transposition
Variant 3

US (federal + states)

Customers whose primary watchlist is US federal or state law. State map, federal agency activity, AG enforcement, pre-emption.

Hero · "What's moving in US privacy + AI" Modules · 50-state law map · federal/state feed Sidebar · in-force snapshot · pre-emption tracker

Shared skeleton — top-bar (search + notifs + profile) · scope switch · hero (title + 3-4 stats) · 2-col body (activity feed left, sidebar right) · watchlist jumper at the foot.

Constraints honoured — no personalisation (scope-default, no per-user prefs) · no performative chrome (no pulses, no "live" dots) · no gradients (solid borders, solid backgrounds) · Source Serif 4 / Inter / IBM Plex Mono · navy + accent blue · 8-region taxonomy on the General jumper.
