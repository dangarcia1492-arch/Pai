AI OECD AI Principles (2019, updated 2024) · Recommendation — non-binding · 47 adherents ⌄
Instrument Recommendation of the Council on Artificial Intelligence (OECD/LEGAL/0449), adopted 22 May 2019; revised 2 May 2024.
Binding No — Recommendation. Adherence is politically expected; not legally enforceable at OECD level.
Adherents 38 OECD members plus 9 non-member adherents (47 total). Principles referenced in EU AI Act recitals and G7 Hiroshima AI Process.

Privacy OECD Privacy Guidelines (1980, revised 2013) · influenced GDPR and most modern regimes ⌄
Instrument Guidelines on the Protection of Privacy and Transborder Flows of Personal Data (1980; revised 2013, OECD/LEGAL/0188).
Binding No — Recommendation. Referenced widely in national law-making; no OECD enforcement mechanism.
Adherents 38 OECD members; framework widely mirrored in GDPR, APEC CBPRs, and national data-protection statutes globally.
