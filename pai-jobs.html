Why a regulatory platform tracks jobs

Because where roles open is where regulatory pressure lands. The Head of AI Governance roles opening at HSBC, Lloyds and BNP tell you what the AI Act is doing to bank governance. The sudden spike in DPO roles across healthcare tells you what DPDPA phase-in looks like in India. Tracking jobs is tracking the labour-market signal of regulation catching up with industry — and it's one of the earliest signals we see.
