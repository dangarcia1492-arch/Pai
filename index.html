FAQ

Answers before you ask.

How often is PAI updated?+

Continuously. Our coverage engine runs 24/7 across every regulator, court, and ministry. Material moves are flagged within hours. The morning brief summarises what shifted overnight in your jurisdictions.

What jurisdictions are covered?+

270+ jurisdictions. Every EU member state, US state, Canadian province, German Bundesland, UK & Crown Dependencies. Major APAC, MENA and LatAm economies. International bodies: OECD, Council of Europe, EDPB, G7, African Union.

What happens after the 7-day free trial?+

Nothing automatic. We don't take a card to start the trial, so nothing charges if you do nothing. You'll see a gentle prompt on day 5 if you want to continue. Annual plans carry a 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions.

Where is my data stored?+

EU only (Hetzner, Germany). Our subprocessor register is in the Trust centre. No behavioural tracking, no analytics cookies, no session replay.

How is this different from subscribing to regulator mailing lists?+

Three differences: Convergence — we track privacy, AI and cyber together, so you see the connections a DPA-only list misses. Interpretation — raw regulator posts don't say what they mean for your org; our editors do. Global — 270+ jurisdictions in one place; subscribing to 200 mailing lists isn't sustainable.
