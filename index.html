Automated decision-making

GDPR Art. 22 EU · UK

A decision based solely on automated processing — including profiling — that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects the data subject.

From the founder

I built PrinciplesAI because I couldn't find it.

When I started tracking regulatory change seriously, I kept hitting the same problem: three siloed sources — one for privacy, one for AI, one for cyber — and none of them spoke to each other. Yet practitioners' work had quietly collapsed into one role covering all three.

So I built what I wanted to read. A live record of every serious body that matters across these three domains. Ranked by what moves the needle for practitioners, not by chronology. Interpreted by local experts who've practiced in those rooms — not aggregated from anonymous wire feeds.

If you're covering all three on one desk, this is for you. If you're not yet, you will be.
