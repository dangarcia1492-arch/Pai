Biography

Practice and focus

Edsel is a Senior Partner of Gorriceta Africa Cauton & Saavedra and heads the Firm’s Data Privacy, Cybersecurity, and AI Initiatives practice group. He also heads the Special Projects group.

His practice focuses on data privacy & cybersecurity, special projects, infrastructure law, technology media & telecommunications, banking and financial services, general corporate services, government procurement under R.A. 9184, and public policy. He is a legal adviser to Fortune 500 and NASDAQ-listed companies and many of the most impactful technology companies in Southeast Asia.

Edsel serves as a Data Protection Officer for leading startups and incumbents. He advises on regulatory aspects of financial technology, cryptocurrency and blockchain-based initiatives, e-commerce, and financial services. He routinely assists clients in setting up a business presence in the Philippines.

Public service & career

Across all three branches of Philippine government

Before joining the Firm, Edsel worked in all three branches of Philippine government — the executive, legislative, and judiciary. He briefly worked as an attorney at SyCip Salazar Hernandez & Gatmaitan.
